About

Hi, I'm Rui.

A passionate tech enthusiast with a knack for simplifying complex technology, and years of hands-on experience in tech support and IT management.

I'm dedicated to helping individuals and small businesses navigate the ever-evolving world of technology — making it accessible, understandable and empowering for everyone.

Learn it. Master it. Or let us handle it.

RS Tech Solutions started from a simple idea: good technology support shouldn't come with a language barrier. Too many people get talked down to, sold things they don't need, or left waiting for a callback that never comes.

So I set out to do it differently — practical, honest advice in plain English, whether you want to understand a problem yourself or hand it over entirely. From a one-off fix to a full support contract, the aim is the same: keep your technology working so you don't have to think about it.

Alongside the day-to-day work, I write guides and tips on the blog — sharing what I learn so you can stay informed, secure and productive.
